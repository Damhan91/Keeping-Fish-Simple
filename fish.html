Choosing your fish

Selecting your fish for your new aquarium is the most exciting part of setting up a new tank. However, for begginers the choice can be overwhelming, as there are so many choices to choose from and not every fish can live peacefully together. To help you through this selction process, we have set out a list of fish that we think is great for begginers.
